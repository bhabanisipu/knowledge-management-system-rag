1. Company Overview
------------------------------------------------------------

Working Days:

------------------------------------------------------------
2. Attendance Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees must mark attendance before 9:30 AM.

Late arrivals exceeding 15 minutes will be considered late attendance.

Three late attendances in a month will be treated as one half-day leave.

Employees are expected to maintain at least 95% attendance every month.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. Leave Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees receive the following paid leaves every year:

12 Casual Leaves

10 Sick Leaves

15 Earned Leaves

Public holidays are announced every year by the HR department.

Employees must apply for leave through the HR portal at least two days before the leave date.

Emergency leave can be approved by the reporting manager.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. Work From Home Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees may work from home up to two days per week with manager approval.

Remote employees must be available during office hours.

Daily stand-up meetings are mandatory.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. Dress Code
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees should wear business casual attire.

Formal dress is mandatory during client meetings.

Identity cards must be worn inside the office premises.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. Code of Conduct
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees should maintain professional behavior.

Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.

Discrimination based on race, religion, gender, or nationality will not be tolerated.

Company property should be handled responsibly.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. IT Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees should not share company passwords.

Official laptops must be password protected.

Software installation requires approval from the IT department.

USB devices from unknown sources are prohibited.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. Performance Review
------------------------------------------------------------

Performance reviews are conducted twice every year.

Evaluation criteria include:

Technical Skills

Communication

Teamwork

Innovation

Problem Solving

Employees with outstanding performance may receive promotions and bonuses.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. Salary Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Salary is credited on the last working day of every month.

Income tax deductions follow government regulations.

Employees receive annual salary revisions based on performance.

------------------------------------------------------------
10. Travel Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Business travel requires manager approval.

Flight tickets should be booked through the company's travel portal.

Hotel reimbursement is limited to ₹5000 per night.

Food reimbursement is limited to ₹1000 per day.

------------------------------------------------------------
11. Security Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees should lock their computers before leaving their desks.

Confidential company information must not be shared with external parties.

Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for company accounts.

------------------------------------------------------------
12. Resignation Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Employees must provide 60 days' notice before resignation.

Exit interviews are mandatory.

Company assets must be returned before the last working day.
